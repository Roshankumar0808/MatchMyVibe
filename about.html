Motivation behind this Web App

This web app is useful for all those people who generally have 15-20 songs that they play on repeat . I am actually one of them who has 15-20 songs playlist and will like to listen those again and again on repeat .So if I start listening one song and liked it I like to listen it on loop. But what encorageed me to make this web app are those ADs that many song streaming platform play after every second song,Which I think no one likes. Yes I know many people will say that we have pre installed music player but actually there are a lot of songs of many artist which are not available on them .So under these circumstances I finally decided to make my own music streaming platform with sequencial collection of only those songs which I actually like to listen and on long run I think so that it will save a lot of time.
